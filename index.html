SPARK-facedetection

Scalability, Pricing And Robustness benchmarK for face detection

View on GitHub

Description

FDDB and MALF are benchmarks for face detection in the wild (in unconstrained settings). On benchmarks for face detection in the lab (in constrained settings, such as constrained scalability and robustness) always, both evaluation criterion precision and recall. benchmark for face detection in the use (in constrained precision and recall), evaluation criterion price scalability on the number of detected faces and robustness against small face size, yaw/pitch/roll, facial expression, occlusion, shadow. We

  1. the most comprehensive collection of benchmarks for face detection in the lab and face detection in the use (8 benchmarks), and
  2. the most comprehensive list of commercialized face detection (total 61).

This could help people purchase face detection.
